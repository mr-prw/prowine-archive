Customer Credit Limit Warning On Confirm Sales Order and Customer Invoice

This module is used to check customer credit limit and if credit limit is less it does not allow to approve Sale Order.

Click here for the video tutorial


Customer Credit Limit Warning On Confirm Sales Order


Configure Customer Credit Limit

Here customer can set the credit limit, If Allow Override is set as true then customer can buy more than its credit limit.



Use of Customer Credit limit on Sales Order

If you try to confirm sale order for a customer whose credit limit is exceeded then error message will be appear.



Change Customer Credit Limit

If Allow Override is set as true then customer can buy more than its credit limit and total amount will be set as customer's new credit limit.




Use of Customer Credit limit on Customer Invoice

If you try to validate customer invoice for a customer whose credit limit is exceeded then error message will be appear.
